Who Were the Light Refractory Beings?

For millennia, the Light Refractory Beings — or Aurivians, as they called themselves — lived in harmony with the fabric of light itself. They believed every color carried a purpose: red for courage, blue for wisdom, and white for unity. Their mastery over light refraction allowed them to build radiant cities, create energy from color, and weave luminous threads into every facet of life.

Highlights:

  • Origins: Emerging from the Prism Epoch, the Aurivians adapted to live in crystalline habitats.
  • Society: Organized into Spectral Houses, each devoted to a color principle.
  • Decline: Legends tell of the Great Dimming — an age when their light fractured and darkness spread.
